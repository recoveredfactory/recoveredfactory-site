Recovered Factory began as a notebook full of sketches, readings, and small experiments.
We wanted a place that felt calm, deliberate, and a little stubborn about craft.

This site is where we share what we are making, what we are learning, and the tiny rituals
that keep the work honest. It is not a feed. It is a workshop table.

If you are reading this, you are early. Thanks for joining us at the start.
